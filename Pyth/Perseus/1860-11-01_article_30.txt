#TEXT: ;;; Sentenced for life.;;; --In the Court of Over and Terminer at New York, Monday, Adam Jaeger, a tailor, convicted of the murder of Wm. Pebble, was sentenced to imprisonment for life.;;; Edward Hagdon, for the murder of John Cunningham, shared the same rate.;;; Both crimes were committed in quarrels, and resulted from the practice of carrying the knife.;;; Patrick Hennessy, fifty years of age, was sentenced to ten years imprisonment for firing his own store in order to obtain the insurance.;;;
