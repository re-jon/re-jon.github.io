#TEXT: ;;; Commissioner's sale of Lot. In the county of Henrico, in the town of Sydney.;;; --As Commissioner appointed by a decree of the Circuit Court of Henrico, pronounced on the 1st November, 1859, in the case of Drummond, Trustee for Ayres, against Keins, I shall proceed to sell at public auction, on the premises, on Saturday, the 10th November, 1860, at 4 o'clock P. M., (if fair; if not the next fair day, except Sunday,) the following real estate, belonging to Jno. W. Reins.;;; as appears by the Commissioner's books and referred to in the plaintiff's bill, viz: "half-Acre", adjoining Richard Reins, two miles from Court House.;;; Terms.--One-third cash; balance at 6 and 12 months, for negotiable notes, satisfactorily endorsed, and title retained till all the purchase money is paid.;;; George W. Randolph.;;;  oc 30--td Commissioner.;;; Sale conducted by Goddin &amp; Apperson.;;;
