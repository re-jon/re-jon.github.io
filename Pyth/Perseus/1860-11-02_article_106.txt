#TEXT: ;;; Paving.;;; --The work of paving Marshall street is going forward, and its completion will add much to the conveniences of that popular thoroughfare.;;; In the meantime, we would call the attention of the authorities to the condition of 5th street, near its intersection with Marshall.;;; This street was in good condition before it was dug up for sewerage purposes; but any one who has passed along that way since the late rain, will agree with us that it now needs paving.;;; The eastern sidewalk of the same street might also be included in the suggestion.;;;
