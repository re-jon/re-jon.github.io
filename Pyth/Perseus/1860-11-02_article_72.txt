#TEXT: ;;; An Emperor in a basket.;;; --The Emperor of Brazil has taken a tour of personal inspection, to observe the progress of the international improvements of his empire.;;; Visiting the Don Pedro Railroad, he rode through one of the tunnels and descended several shafts.;;; In one basket sat the Emperor, and in the other Major Ellison, who was selected from the suite for this perilous honor because he was nearest the Emperor's size and weight.--The result of the exploration was that the contractors were allowed an extension of time, the granite cuttings being exceedingly difficult.;;;
