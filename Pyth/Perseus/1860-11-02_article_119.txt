#HEADER: Handsome dividends.
#TEXT: ;;; Handsome dividends.;;; As will be seen by an advertisement in another column, the Board of DirFire and Marine Insurance Company have declared a dividend of ten per cent. Out of the profits of the last six months, payable to the stockholders on the 15th inst., thus showing that twenty per cent, have been delayed for the past twelve months. With such dividends, it is not to be wondered that the stock commands a high figure at all times, and that very little, if any, of it is to be found in the market.;;; We think the dividends speak for the management in the highest terms of commendation.;;;
