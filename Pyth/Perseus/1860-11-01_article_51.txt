#TEXT: ;;; Music.;;; --The Armory Band yesterday went about the streets in two wagons, and entertained the citizens with some very good music.;;; As there was no placard announcing the "show" or a race to come off, we take it for granted that the Band was giving a concert gratis, for which, in behalf of the public, we return thanks.;;;
