New Orleans, Oct. 31.;;; --The steamer De Soto, from Havana on the 27th, reports the Havana sugar market unchanged.;;; The growing cane crop is in excellent condition.;;; An arrival from Vera Cruz says the $400,000 seized in the specie conducta by Delgado had been returned to its owners.;;;
