#TEXT: ;;; Jolly Fellows.;;; --More of those jolly chaps who spend two-thirds of their time in the dram shops and the other third in the gutters, were deposited in the watch-house on Wednesday night. One of them, named Samuel Allen, had been discharged but a few hours previous, when the officers discovered him taking up more room on the sidewalk than properly belonged to two or three.;;; The Mayor sent him to jail this time.;;; The vice of intemperance has so many votaries, that the city will soon be at a loss for accommodations, and the Council may make a move in the matter of a new jail.;;;
