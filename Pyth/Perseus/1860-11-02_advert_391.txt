#TEXT: ;;; Kentucky Mules for sale at Auction.;;; --I will expose to public sale, on Saturday, 3d of November, at 11 o'clock, at the stables of Messrs Johnson &amp; Blankenship, on Franklin, near Wall street, fifteen likely young Kentucky Mules, on a credit of three months, for approved paper.;;; Those wanting first class Mules, would do well to be present, as this opportunity seldom occurs, as my business calls me from this city, and I am determined to sell.;;; These Mules I procured to suit this market.--They are of fine size, some of which are sixteen hands high; are in fine condition, and I can, with confidence, recommend them.;;; They can be seen at the above stables until day of sale.;;; no 2--2t* George Ferguson.;;;
