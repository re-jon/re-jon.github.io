#TEXT: ;;; Death of a naval officer.;;; --Lieut. John T. Barraud, of the U. S. Navy, died at Portsmouth, Va., very suddenly, Sunday night.--He was a native of this State, and entered the service in 1841 as a midshipman.;;; He was stationed at the Gosport Navy-Yard.;;;
