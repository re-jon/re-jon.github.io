#TEXT: ;;; Suicide.;;; --Mrs. James Watkins, residing near Alexandria, Va., committed suicide on the 29th ult. by taking strychnine.;;; The deceased had been for some time in a very low-spirited state of mind, had been heard to make threats against her own life, and at different times had purchased laudanum with a view to self-destruction.;;;
