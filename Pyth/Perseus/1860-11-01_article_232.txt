#TEXT: ;;;   Lynchburg, Va.,Oct. 31.;;; -- The storm of the  two days has greatly damaged the Virginia and Tennessee Railroad. Twelve bridges were swept away, and numerous land slides occurred.;;; The road bed is washed up and destroyed for miles.;;; The full extent of the damage is not known, but the lose cannot be less than $150,000. Some weeks must elapse before it can be repaired sufficiently for the passage of trains, and passengers are now returning to take other routes.;;; Preparations are making to run a stage line along the railroad route.;;;
