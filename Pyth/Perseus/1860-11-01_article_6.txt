#TEXT: ;;; Killed.;;; --James Brooks, baggage master on the Nashville and Chattanooga Railroad, was killed near Murfreesboro', Tenn., on the 26th ult. He was standing on the top of a car as the train passed beneath a bridge, when his head struck the lower beam of the bridge, and he was instantly killed.;;;
