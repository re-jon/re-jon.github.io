#TEXT: ;;; The Encampment.;;; --A company of cavalry in Caroline county having signified an intention of coming to the encampment, there will be sixteen companies in the field on that occasion.;;; The Governor's Guard and the Henrico Light Dragoons were parading yesterday on the old Fair Grounds, and were instructed in the various movements of squadron drill.;;; The preparations for the encampment are attracting attention in other quarters of the country, and the result will prove that Virginia is not unmindful of her interests.;;;
