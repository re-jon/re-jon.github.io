#TEXT: ;;; Selling the Prince's soiled Linen.;;; --The Montreal papers notice the approaching sale of the plate, glass and linen which was used by the Prince of Wales during his stay in that city.;;; The property comprises some beautifully fine sheets and other bed linen, table and toilette linen of equally beautiful quality, very delicate glassware of all kinds, and a beautiful set of the finest description of electroplate, including every article required for the table.;;;
