#TEXT: ;;; The stolen valice.;;; --The negro boy Harrison, charged with stealing A. A. White's valice, at the St. Charles Hotel, was before the Mayor yesterday, when a message came that the wrong darkey had been arrested.;;; Harrison, who was somewhat surprised at the arrest, declares that he "don't know nuthin' about de bodice," and from all accounts, the negro who pointed him out to the officer made a mistake.;;;
