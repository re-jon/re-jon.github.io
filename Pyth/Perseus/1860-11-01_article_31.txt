#TEXT: ;;; Awful accident.;;; --On Saturday last John Hardon, the proprietor of the "Morgan Steam Saw--Mill," about three miles from Georgetown, Del., was superintending the operations of a circular saw. By some means his foot slipped, throwing him directly in contact with the saw, which passed through his body in a few revolutions, severing the upper half from the lower, and throwing the heart, liver and entrails of the unfortunate man in all directions over the mill.;;;
