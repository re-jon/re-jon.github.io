#TEXT: ;;; The Massachusetts Fisheries.;;; --The fishing fleet has this year, as a whole, done poorly.;;; Several of the Labrador vessels lost money.--In the Bay mackereling, a few vessels obtaining full fares will do remarkably well, as they must when mackerel sell at $16 a barrel; but more of them with a small catch will barely pay their way, and others will be unable to meet their bills.;;; The shore fishing has been equally uncertain.;;; Those that struck the mackerel that for a time swarmed about the coast of Maine, and a few engaged in taking pollock for the last three weeks, will find a good balance of profits in their favor.--Newburyport Herald.;;;
