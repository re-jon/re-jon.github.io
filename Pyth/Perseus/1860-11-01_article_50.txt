Prisoners.;;; --The crew of the slaver Storm-King, thirteen in number, captured on the coast of Africa, will be brought to this city in a day or two from Norfolk, to await their trial in the Federal Court.;;; As the city jail is pretty well filled up, it is probable that the county jail will have to be resorted to for the safe-keeping of these prisoners.;;;
