#TEXT: ;;; A Grand promenade.;;; --The inhabitants of Paris are to be gratified with another magnificent promenade.;;; The Emperor has given orders for the construction of a road around Paris, within the fortifications.;;; It will be twenty miles long and one hundred and twenty feet wide.;;; The footpaths will be bordered with trees.;;;
