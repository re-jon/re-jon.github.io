#TEXT: ;;; The coming Conflict.;;; --The Bell and Everett men of Richmond are working hard to give a strong support to their candidates on the day of election.;;; It will be seen by the announcement in to-day's paper that the Club House will be open on Monday next, and that several speakers will address the people at night.;;; The two wings of the Democracy are working with no less ardor, and all parties will marshal their forces in full strength on Tuesday next.;;;
