#TEXT: ;;; Burned to death.;;; --Two children of Ed. Wooster, of Falls Village, Conn., were burned to death last week.;;; The mother left the house for a short time, and locked in three children, who, while playing, accidentally set the house on fire, and became so frightened that they went to bed in a chamber ever the fire.;;; When the fire was discovered one end of the house was mostly burnt, and one of the children was found dead and a second so badly burned that it died the same evening.;;;
