#TEXT: ;;; Seventy-Five Dollars reward.;;; --I will pay $7500 for the apprehension of my Negro Girl, Peggy, who absconded about three weeks ago. She is a low thick set mulatto, with short curly hair, and is about sixteen years old. Her dress cannot be described, as she has been seen several times in this city since she left, and had on a different dress each time.   E. L.  Chinn.  Richmond, Oct. 25. 1860. oc 26--ts;;;
