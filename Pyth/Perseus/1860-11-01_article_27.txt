#TEXT: ;;; Marine disaster.;;; --A mackerel schooner, from Gloucester, Mass., was recently rundown by a steamer in a dense fog, near Cape Canso, and, after floating two days waterlogged, seven of the crew were washed overboard.;;; The remaining nine took a boat, which capsized, and two men were devoured by sharks.;;; The remaining seven recovered the boat, and two more died of exhaustion.;;; The survivors were rescued by a Portuguese vessel and carried to St. Domingo, where the American Consul treated them kindly and would send them home.;;; No names are given.;;;
