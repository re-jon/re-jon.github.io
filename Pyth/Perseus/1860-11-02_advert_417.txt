#HEADER: Commissioner's sale of a Lot in the town of Sydney, on the West side of Plumb, between Elmwood and Dover streets.
#TEXT: ;;; Commissioner's sale of a Lot in the town of Sydney, on the West side of Plumb, between Elmwood and Dover streets.;;; --As Commissioner appointed by a decree of the Circuit Court of Henrico, pronounced on the 8th November, 1859, in the case of "Degenhardt. &amp;c., against Thacker, &amp;c.," I shall proceed to sell at public auction, on the premises, on Monday, the 5th November, 1860 at 4 o'clock P. M., (if fair; if not, the next fair day,) the Real Estate purchased by Joseph Saunders, at a sale made on the 13th August, 1858, by Messrs. P. Roberts and W. W. Crump, Commissioners, and which he has failed to pay for, viz: A Lot, located as above, fronting 55 feet on the west side of Plumb street, running back 70 feet.;;; Terms.--One-third cash; balance at 6 and 12 months, for negotiable notes, interest added, and title retained till all the purchase money is paid.;;; W. Goddin, Com'r,;;; Goddin &amp; Apperson, Aucts. oc 16--tds;;;
